Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 50 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 0.5 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 eggs and 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs and milk, then whisk into a smooth batter.
  4. Pour batter into a greased baking tray.
  5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes and let it cool before serving.
