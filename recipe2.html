Biryani

Preparation Time: 60 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 300 g chicken or vegetables
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup curd
  • Biryani masala, salt, and oil

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Cook onions in oil until golden.
  3. Add chicken or vegetables and spices, then cook well.
  4. Add curd and mix for a few minutes.
  5. Layer rice and masala, then cook on low heat until done.
