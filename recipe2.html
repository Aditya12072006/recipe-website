Biryani

Preparation Time: 60 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 300 g chicken or vegetables
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup curd
  • Biryani masala, salt, and oil

Steps

  1. Rinse basmati rice 2 to 3 times until the water is mostly clear, then soak it in fresh water for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy-bottom pot, add sliced onions, and fry on medium heat until they turn deep golden brown.
  3. Remove half the fried onions for garnishing later, and keep the remaining onions in the pot.
  4. Add chicken or vegetables with biryani masala and salt, then cook for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add curd and mix thoroughly so the masala becomes creamy and coats the chicken or vegetables evenly.
  6. Cook this masala on low heat for 5 to 7 minutes until oil starts separating at the edges.
  7. In a separate pot, boil water with a little salt and cook the soaked rice until it is about 70% cooked, then drain.
  8. Spread half the rice over the masala layer, then add some reserved fried onions and repeat with the remaining rice.
  9. Cover the pot tightly and cook on very low heat for 15 to 20 minutes (dum) so the rice finishes cooking in steam.
  10. Turn off heat, rest for 5 minutes, fluff gently with a fork, and serve hot.
