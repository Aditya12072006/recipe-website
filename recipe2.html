Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 50 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 0.5 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 eggs and 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 C for at least 10 minutes so it reaches a stable baking temperature.
  2. Grease a cake tin with a little oil or butter and dust lightly with flour to prevent sticking.
  3. In a large bowl, sieve flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder to remove lumps and aerate the dry mix.
  4. Add sugar to the dry ingredients and whisk gently until everything is evenly combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and milk together until smooth.
  6. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and whisk until you get a lump-free, pourable batter.
  7. Do not overmix; once the batter looks smooth and glossy, stop whisking.
  8. Transfer the batter to the prepared tin and tap it gently on the counter to release trapped air bubbles.
  9. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  10. Cool the cake in the tin for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely before slicing and serving.
