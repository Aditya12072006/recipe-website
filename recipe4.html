Salad

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (grated)
  • Lemon juice and salt

Steps

  1. Rinse cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and carrot thoroughly under running water to remove dirt.
  2. Peel the cucumber and carrot if needed, then chop cucumber and tomatoes into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Slice the onion thinly so it mixes evenly without overpowering each bite.
  4. Grate or julienne the carrot for better texture and color in the salad.
  5. Place all chopped vegetables in a large mixing bowl.
  6. Add salt and freshly squeezed lemon juice little by little instead of all at once.
  7. Toss gently with two spoons so vegetables stay crisp and do not become mushy.
  8. Taste and adjust with more lemon juice or salt based on your preference.
  9. Let the salad rest for 2 to 3 minutes so flavors blend lightly.
  10. Serve immediately while fresh and crunchy.
