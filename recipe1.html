Pasta

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water, add 1 teaspoon salt, and bring it to a rolling boil over high heat.
  2. Add the pasta and stir immediately for 20 to 30 seconds so it does not stick to the bottom.
  3. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente, then reserve 1/2 cup pasta water and drain the rest.
  4. Place a wide pan on medium heat, add olive oil, then add chopped garlic and saute for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, stir well, and let the sauce simmer for 4 to 5 minutes to reduce slightly.
  6. If the sauce becomes too thick, add 2 to 3 tablespoons reserved pasta water and mix to get a silky texture.
  7. Add the drained pasta to the sauce and toss continuously for 2 minutes so every piece is coated.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning with extra salt or pepper if needed.
  9. Turn off heat, let the pasta rest for 1 minute, then serve hot.
