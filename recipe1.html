Pasta

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft and drain it.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saute garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix boiled pasta with sauce and cook for 2 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
