Dosa

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Potato masala (optional)
  • Chutney for serving

Steps

  1. Heat a flat pan and lightly grease it with oil.
  2. Pour a ladle of batter and spread in circular motion.
  3. Drizzle a little oil around the dosa.
  4. Cook until golden brown and crisp.
  5. Fold and serve with chutney.
